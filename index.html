What we do

We provide extremely low-cost, safe bicycle helmets to users of bicycle-share programs.

Safe

Our helmets are thoroughly tested in-house and elsewhere to ensure they can protect your head in a crash. Furthermore, we pass Consumer Product Safety Commission standard for bicycle helmets.

Low-cost

We want to ensure everyone is able to access a helmet when using bicycle-sharing programs, so it's key our helmets are inexpensive.

Biodegradeable

Our helmets are design to decay over the course of 30 days once disposed.
